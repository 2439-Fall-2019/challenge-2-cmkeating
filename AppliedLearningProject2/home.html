Sneaker Culture

The purpose of this application is to help facilitate an interest in Sneaker culture and somewhat explain why there is such a fanatic fanbase surrounding shoes. To the outside world it is a very odd thing to have a fanatic interest in. This app will explain the culture using personal experience and help to guide you to purchasing your own cool pair.

My Collection: This page will show my personal pairs and why I like them so much. An inside look into my spending decisions if you will.

Find your own pair: This page will help you to make an informed decision on where you can find a reliable seller and can find your own pair.

Survey: I ask that you take this survey so that I can improve the page and gather information on if the goal of my application was succesful.
